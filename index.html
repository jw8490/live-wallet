The SSA Ecosystem Cap is a custom dashboard metric representing the combined market value, demand momentum, platform activity, and consumer attention signals tracked inside Shop Share Anywhere. It is designed to frame SSA as an index layer over commerce, social, AI, fintech, and prediction-market platforms.
